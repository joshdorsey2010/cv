Joshua G Dorsey

Inventor of Sit and Decompress therapy and Posturific Brace a figure 8 posture brace.

Born in 1979 in the small town of Amsterdam, Ohio. Amsterdam is known for coal mining and steel mills. I attended Edison School District, played three sports and was also part of choir and ski club. School was enjoyable and seemed to go by too fast.

After highschool I attended Kent State University. While at Kent, I played baseball and ended up changing my studies from computers to chiropractic. From there I went to chiropractic college in Whittier, Ca. After graduating from chiropractic college in 2003 I moved back to Ohio to practice and be closer to my family.
